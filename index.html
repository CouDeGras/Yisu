Meet the Man Behind His Own Website

Hello there! I’m Yisu Fang—a robotics engineer, IoT specialist, and, most recently, the creative mind behind this very website. If you’re here, you’ve probably come across my work in robotics, artificial intelligence, or IoT. Or perhaps you just wanted to meet the person who turned his CV into a newsworthy website. Either way, welcome! A Foundation of Curiosity My academic journey began with a BEng in Internet of Things Engineering with Contemporary Entrepreneurialism from Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU), awarded by the University of Liverpool. Graduating with First Class Honours, I combined a passion for technology with an entrepreneurial mindset. Fast forward to today, I’m pursuing an MSc in Robotics at the University of Birmingham, where I’m diving deep into autonomous systems, AI-powered decision-making, and IoT integration. The Work I’m Proud Of I thrive on solving real-world challenges with innovative solutions. From developing an autonomous irrigation robot powered by ROS2 and YOLOv8 to creating a secure, distributed robotic control system with AES-encrypted ESP32 wireless mesh networks, I’ve built projects that bridge cutting-edge technology and practical applications. My work often finds its way into academic circles, with publications at esteemed conferences like ICiMR and IEEE SmartIoT. Highlights include designing SLAM-based robotic navigation systems and crafting NLP-powered robots that understand and execute multilingual voice commands. The Skillset That Drives Me Behind every project is a diverse skill set: proficiency in ROS2, SLAM, Python, embedded systems, and AI algorithms, to name a few. Whether it’s optimizing machine vision algorithms or developing IoT applications, my aim is always to push boundaries while maintaining a focus on real-world impact. Outside the Lab You might find me tinkering with DIY robotics projects, creating concept art in Unreal Engine 5, or contemplating the retrofuturist aesthetics of a reimagined Roman Empire. (Yes, I dabble in world-building for video games, too!) Why This Website? This website is more than a portfolio; it’s a glimpse into how I think and work. The same creativity and precision that brought you this article guide my approach to research and innovation. So, whether you’re a prospective supervisor, collaborator, or someone intrigued by the fusion of tech and storytelling, feel free to explore. And if you’d like to connect, my inbox is always open.

Graduating with First Class Honours wasn’t just about acing exams; it was about immersing myself in the principles of IoT, diving headfirst into entrepreneurship, and learning to bridge the gap between technical know-how and practical application. What Set This Degree Apart Here’s the thing about IoT: it’s not just about sensors and servers; it’s about creating intelligent systems that talk to each other—and, more importantly, to us. Throughout my degree, I tackled challenges that tested my skills and stretched my creativity. And the entrepreneurial aspect wasn’t just theoretical. Whether it was pitching ideas for smart systems or collaborating on IoT-based solutions, I developed a knack for thinking beyond the code—toward innovation, impact, and scalability. Where It’s Taken Me This “first class” engineer-ing degree set the stage for my current pursuits: an MSc in Robotics at the University of Birmingham, where I’m exploring the integration of SLAM, ROS2, and AI-powered decision-making for autonomous systems.
